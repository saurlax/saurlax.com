# Project Gecynd

Gecynd is an extraordinary voxel sandbox game that offers a more refined
and sophisticated gaming experience. It operates on modular components,
allowing players to personalize their game through lua coding.

With Gecynd, you will be able to revel in a breathtaking natural
environment filled with realistic geological formations, such as majestic
mountains and vast water bodies, all in accordance with the laws of
physics.Moreover, the game boasts of its weather system and universe
system, providing players with a true spherical planetary system that
simulates the changes in seasons and the cycle of day and night.
Additionally, the game features a wealth of exploration opportunities,
from biology to chemistry, and even authentic physics simulations.

You are in for a truly novel and engaging gaming experience with Gecynd.
